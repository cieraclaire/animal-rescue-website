Rescues

Immerse yourself into the life changing stories we have experienced here at the Oxford Animal Rescue. Through years of trials and tribulation, we let you embark on the journey with us to saving each and every animal we come across and providing care to them to give them to the perfect family.

Donate

Each donation made goes into helping to keep the shelter afoot and providing proper maintenance to ensure safety and security to the animals housed at Oxford Animal Rescue. Proceeds will also go to help in the fight for the stigma against Mental Health!

Adopt

Adopting a pet from Oxford Animal Rescue will bring you happiness with the love and care we have provided for your new family member. The love we have given them will reflect from them and onto you as you grow with each other. Adopt your new family member today!
